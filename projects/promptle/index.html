01. The Game

Promptle is a daily game for the AI era. Players are presented with a unique, AI-generated image and must deduce the exact 5-word prompt used to generate it. Using color-coded feedback similar to Wordle, players refine their guesses to find the semantic match.

02. The Challenge

Designing Promptle meant walking a fine line: keeping the mechanics simple while ensuring the gameplay remained interesting. The main hurdle was tuning the difficulty to a satisfying "medium"—ensuring AI-generated images were clear enough to decipher, yet abstract enough to require genuine deduction. We iterated extensively on the feedback system to guide players without handing them the answer.
