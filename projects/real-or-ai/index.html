Test your visual intuition. Distinguish reality from synthesis in "Real or AI", or spot generated alterations in "Find the Difference".

01. Overview

"REAL OR AI?" is a dual-mode puzzle game designed to train your eye in the age of generative media. In the primary mode, users must distinguish between high-quality photography and AI synthesis. In "Find the Difference", the challenge shifts to identifying subtle AI manipulations within real scenes.

02. Key Features

  • Dual Game Modes: Switch between "Real vs AI" authentication and "Find the Difference" alteration spotting.
  • Daily Challenges: A curated set of 5 pairs every day. Compete for the high score on the global leaderboard.
  • Upload & Verify: Users can upload their own photos to test the community's perception limits.
  • AI Inpainting: Advanced masking algorithms subtly alter user photos for the "Find the Difference" mode.

03. The Challenges

Image Generation: The core difficulty lay in generating "similar studios" for the comparison mode. We needed the AI to produce images that were stylistically close enough to real photos to be deceptive, without being obvious fakes.

Masking & Inpainting: For the "Find the Difference" mode, the challenge was technically complex: developing a system to automatically identifying objects in user uploads, masking them out, and seamlessly generating new AI elements that blend perfectly with the original lighting and perspective.
