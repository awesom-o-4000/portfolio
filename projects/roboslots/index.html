  • Website View Live

Spin, build, and battle. Win parts to construct unique mechanical fighters, then enter the AI-generated arena where victory and defeat are visualized in custom battle scenes.

01. Overview

RoboSlots combines the thrill of a slot machine with the strategy of a robot battler. Players spin to collect resources, assemble custom mechs from hundreds of parts, and test their creations in an AI-simulated arena. It challenges the traditional concept of "loot" by making every part a visual component of your fighter.

02. Key Features

  • Slot Economy: Spin for resources. Win common scrap or legendary components to upgrade your build.
  • AI Robot Generation: Every robot is uniquely generated based on the parts you equip and your battle history.
  • Battle Arena: Pit your creation against others. The outcome is determined by stats but visualized by AI.
  • Dynamic Match Results: Winners are shown standing triumphant; losers are depicted in defeat. No two screens are alike.

03. The Challenges

Game Design: Integrating a slot machine mechanic with an RPG-style battle system regarding economy balance was complex. We had to ensure that spinning felt rewarding (`win gold coins and robot parts`) without breaking the progression curve of the arena battles.

Consistency: Maintaining a consistent visual style for the AI-generated robots was critical. Since match results are generated separately for winners (victory pose) and losers (defeated on the ground), we needed strict prompt engineering to ensure the "same" robot appeared in both states.
