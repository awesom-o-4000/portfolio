Gen AI creators platform and marketplace.

01. Overview

Prompt & Pixel is the premier platform and marketplace for the Generative AI era. It bridges the gap between creativity and commerce, allowing artists to showcase their portfolios and monetize their work through direct asset sales or prompt licensing. Simultaneously, it serves as a talent hub where clients can list projects and hire top-tier Gen AI artists—effectively merging the visual discovery of Dribbble with the professional gig economy of Upwork.

02. Key Features

  • Marketplace: A flexible commerce layer where buyers can choose between purchasing usage rights for the final image or acquiring the source prompt to generate their own variations.
  • Portfolio Showcase: A dedicated, visually immersive space for creators to display their best AI work.
  • Talent Matchmaking: Clients can post jobs and hire specialized AI artists for bespoke projects.
  • Community: A hub for creators to connect, share techniques, and stay updated on the latest models.

03. The Challenges

Building a dual-sided marketplace required a robust reputation system to ensure quality and trust in a rapidly evolving field. We implemented verification flows for artists and secure transaction handling for both asset purchases and freelance contracts to protect both buyers and creators.
