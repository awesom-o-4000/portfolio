AI sticker maker and set generator. Create cute 3D stickers from any photo.

01. Overview

Stickeroo is an AI-powered tool that generates cute, plastic-shaded 3D stickers with volumetric lighting—completely without background. Users can upload a photo or snap a picture with their camera, and the app automatically identifies the best processing module: Portrait or Scene.

02. Key Features

  • Portrait Mode: Generates a cute 3D character. If the subject is cropped, the AI interprets and completes a full body.
  • Scene Mode: Extracts main subjects from complex photos and creates a clean isometric 3D model.
  • Set Generation: Create a consistent set of matching stickers based on your generated character.
  • High-Res Output: All stickers are rendered with high-resolution textures and humanized details.

03. The Challenges

The primary challenges in building Stickeroo were defining the specific "cute 3D" look, ensuring consistency across generated sets, and maintaining high output quality. Balancing the AI's creativity with the need for recognizable subject extraction was key to a successful MVP.
