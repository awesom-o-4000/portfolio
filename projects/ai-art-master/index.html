Competitive "Popularity Guessing" game with AI rewards.

01. Overview

AI Art Master is an early-stage mobile game concept that gamifies the art of curation. Inspired by GuruShots' photography challenges, players compete in a fast-paced guessing game: "Which photo is more popular?" Correct guesses accumulate points, driving players forward in a competitive 'race' against opponents to reach a target score.

02. Game Mechanics

  • The Race: A real-time progression system where every correct guess speeds you up. Reaching the target number of right guesses first wins the match.
  • Boosts & Combat: Players can deploy strategic power-ups: Freeze opponents to halt their progress, Shield against incoming attacks, or Boost their own speed.
  • Rich Rewards: Winners earn chests filled with Prompt Packs, Gold Coins, and "Energy" to power their own AI art generation.
  • Venue Levels: The meta-game involves curating your own exhibitions. Each level is a unique venue—from ancient ruins (Past) to modern galleries (Present) and sci-fi stations (Future)—where players hang and showcase their collected or generated art on the walls.

03. The Challenges

Developing this concept with early generative models posed significant hurdles in maintaining established styling. Generating **consistent venues** with matching **image frame designs** required precise composition and prompting. Furthermore, creating a cohesive visual language across **animations**, **illustrations**, and the **onboarding** experience was critical to making the novel gameplay intuitive.
